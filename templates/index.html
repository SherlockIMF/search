What We Do

We designed a semantic search and browse tool that aims to help researchers at IMF with their studies by finding relevant papers, authors and concepts.

In the first version, we developed a powerfull search engine that can find relevant contents for a given query using the state-of-the-art word2vec technologies. In addition, users can find relevant authors for a given topic or concept. We also provide user with a set of recommended papers for further studies based on her paper of interest. Below we showcase different intermediate and final products developed in this project.
